Can you work with our existing Kubernetes clusters?

Absolutely. We work with EKS, AKS, GKE, and self-managed Kubernetes clusters. Our AI-powered assessment scans your existing infrastructure and delivers a health scorecard with dollar-quantified findings - no rip-and-replace required.

What Kubernetes certifications does your team hold?

Our engineers hold CKA (Certified Kubernetes Administrator), CKS (Certified Kubernetes Security Specialist), and CKAD (Certified Kubernetes Application Developer) certifications, backed by 10+ years of production Kubernetes experience.

Do you provide ongoing Kubernetes managed services?

Yes. After the initial assessment or transformation, we offer managed Kubernetes operations, fractional platform team support, and retainer-based K8s consulting. We handle upgrades, security patching, monitoring, and incident response.

How quickly can you reduce our Kubernetes cloud costs?

Our AI agents typically identify 30-50% cost savings within the first 5-10 day assessment. Quick wins like right-sizing, spot instances, and Karpenter autoscaling can be implemented immediately. Full FinOps transformation takes 1-3 months.
