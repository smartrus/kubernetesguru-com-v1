The Global Kubernetes Authority

KubernetesGuru.com is a Kubernetes knowledge hub built by the team at kubernetes.ae - certified K8s engineers with 20+ years of Linux and cloud-native experience. We publish the guides, tools, and frameworks we wish had existed when we were learning.

Why We Built This

The Kubernetes ecosystem produces enormous amounts of documentation, but most of it is scattered, version-ambiguous, or written for people who already know what they’re doing. The CNCF docs explain what a PodDisruptionBudget is; they rarely explain when to use one and what happens if you don’t.

We built KubernetesGuru.com to fill that gap. Every guide here comes from real production deployments - the kind of situations where a misconfigured liveness probe caused a cascading rollback, where a missing LimitRange let a runaway container starve a node, where a ClusterRole bound to a CI service account had cluster-admin because “we’ll fix it later.”

The interactive tools exist because the mental math of Kubernetes cost estimation and RBAC policy generation is tedious enough that people skip it. We didn’t want that. We wanted you to be able to open the Cost Calculator and know in 30 seconds whether your cluster is worth investigating.

What This Site Is For

KubernetesGuru.com is a free resource. We don’t gate the tools, we don’t require a sign-up to read the guides, and we don’t run ads. We’re playing a long game: if this site helps platform engineers solve real problems, some of them will eventually need consulting help - and when they do, they’ll know where to find us.
