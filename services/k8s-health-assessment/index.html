Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the K8s Health Assessment take?

The assessment runs 5-7 days. Days 1-2 cover automated AI-powered scanning of up to 5 clusters, days 3-4 focus on analysis and scoring across 8 dimensions, and days 5-7 deliver the health scorecard, findings report, and prioritized remediation roadmap.

Is the scanning safe for production clusters?

Yes. Our AI agents perform read-only, non-invasive scanning. We run automated checks against CIS Benchmarks, resource utilization metrics, and best practices without making any changes to your cluster configuration or workloads.

What are the 8 dimensions you assess?

We assess cluster configuration, resource management, security posture, networking, observability, backup and disaster recovery, cost efficiency, and operational readiness. Each dimension is scored 1-5 with evidence and dollar amounts attached to every finding.

How do you quantify cost waste?

We analyze resource requests vs. actual utilization at the namespace level and calculate the dollar amount of waste per month. Every cost finding includes a specific before/after YAML diff showing exactly what to change. Typical clusters have 30-50% waste.

What happens after the assessment?

You receive a Quick Wins document with the top 10 fixes for week one, plus a prioritized remediation roadmap from Quick Wins to Strategic improvements. We can also provide implementation support to execute the roadmap if your team needs hands-on help.

Do you map findings to compliance frameworks like SOC2 or CIS Benchmarks?

Yes. We map all Kubernetes security findings to CIS Kubernetes Benchmark v1.8 controls and can provide supplementary mapping to SOC2, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, or GDPR requirements. This gives your compliance team a clear picture of how cluster security posture relates to your regulatory obligations.
